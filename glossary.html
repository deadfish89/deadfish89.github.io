Glossary

There are many terms in League of Legends that may be confusing to newer players. As such, this is a glossary for any terms that are not obvious.

Auto Attack

The default way most champions deal damage. Can be used infinitely but requires an enemy target within range.

ADC

ADC, an abbreviation of Attack Damage Carry, usually refers to a marksman. Can also be used to refer to the position of bottom lane, which is usually also a marksman.

Bottom

Has multiple meanings. Can either refer to the physical bottom lane, or the position of bottom which is usually taken by a marksman.

Burst

Dealing a large amount of damage in a short period of time.

Crowd Control

The ability to impair the movement or actions of enemy champions.

DPS

How much sustained damage a champion can deal, usually only considering basic attacks and low-cooldown abilities.

Gap Closer

An ability that allows a champion to get closer to their enemy

Global

An ability that has extremely long range and can affect anywhere on the map

Jungle

Refers to the area between the lanes filled with camps, or the position who’s objective is to clear the camps.

Kiting

The act of damaging an enemy champion while also running away at the same time.

Lane

One of the three lanes on the map, top, middle or bottom.

Mana

The resource used by most champions to cast spells.

Mid

Abbreviation for middle, either the middle lane or the position of middle.

Peel

The act of using abilities, usually crowd control, to stop enemy champions from attacking allies.

Roam

The act of moving from your lane to other lanes. Usually done only by mid laners and supports.

Scaling

How much a champion's power increases as the game goes longer and they get levels and items.

Skillshot

An ability that needs to be aimed and can be dodged by the enemy.

Squishy

A champion with low health and defenses that is easily killed

Sustain

A champion’s ability to heal consistently.

Tankiness

The ability to take large amounts of damage.

Ult

Short for ultimate ability, which is usually a champion’s most powerful ability and unlocked later than the others.

For a more complete list of terms, go to the League of Legends Wiki.
