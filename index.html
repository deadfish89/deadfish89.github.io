League of Legends

League of Legends is one of the most popular games in the world, and played by millions of people worldwide. However, it can have a steep learning curve. This website aims to teach players about some of the champions so they can play quicker. The champion table has every champion put into classes. The champion guides have short tips for every champion. The class guides have information for each class, and the glossary has definitions for terms that may be unfamiliar.
